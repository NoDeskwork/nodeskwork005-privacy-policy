Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how NoDeskwork ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera and Video

The App uses your device's camera to record videos. Videos are recorded only when you explicitly initiate recording.

Uploaded Content

When you choose to upload videos, they are transmitted to our cloud storage service (Cloudflare). We store these videos to provide the app's functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the recorded videos solely to provide the app's core functionality of video recording and upload services.

Data Storage and Security

Videos are stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We retain uploaded videos until you delete them or close your account.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Choose not to grant camera permissions (the app will not function without them)
  • Delete your uploaded videos at any time
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@nodeskwork.com
