Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how NoDeskwork ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our mobile application, and our AI analysis website at https://app.nodeskwork.com.

Information We Collect

Camera and Video

The App uses your device's camera to record videos. Videos are recorded only when you explicitly initiate recording.

Uploaded Content

Your recorded videos are uploaded to our cloud storage service. You can monitor (or cancel if required) uploads with progress bars on the app's screen. We store these videos to provide AI video analysis service on our website for you.

How We Use Your Information

We use the recorded videos to provide our core functionality of AI analysis services.

Data Storage and Security

Videos are stored securely using industry-standard encryption. You can delete your uploaded videos and AI outputs in one of two ways: directly through the website at https://app.nodeskwork.com, or by sending us a deletion request. we will keep your uploaded videos and AI Outputs until the earliest of the following events: the paid service period ends, one year retention period expires, or your account is closed.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Choose not to grant camera permissions (the app will not function without them)
  • Request deletion of your videos by contacting us

Contact Us
