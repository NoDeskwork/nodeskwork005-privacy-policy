Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how NoDeskwork ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera and Video

The App uses your device's camera to record videos. Videos are recorded only when you explicitly initiate recording.

Uploaded Content

Your recorded videos are uploaded to our cloud storage service. You can monitor (or cancel if required) uploads with progress bars on the app's screen. We store these videos to provide AI video analysis service on our website for you.

How We Use Your Information

We use the recorded videos to provide our core functionality of AI analysis services.

Data Storage and Security

Videos are stored securely using industry-standard encryption. You have two options for deleting uploaded videos: you can do so directly at https://app.nodeskwork.com, or you can request that we delete them for you. Otherwise, we will retain your uploaded videos whichever comes sooner: either the expiration of the retain period or the closure of your account.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Choose not to grant camera permissions (the app will not function without them)
  • Request deletion of your videos by contacting us

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@nodeskwork.com
